Employment
The Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association (LACAAEA) is a non-profit organization organized under the provisions of Internal Revenue Code (IRC) Section 501(c) 3 and an employee organization established to work with the County management and other parties towards achievement of the following goals:
  • Improve the employment status of its members.
  • Encourage the recruitment, appointment and promotion of Asian-Americans in County Service.
  • Disseminate information on employment and promotional opportunities to its members.
  • Counsel and assist its members in matters relating to grievance procedures, affirmative action, race and sex discrimination.
  • Participate in employment related activities conducted by Asian-American communities.
