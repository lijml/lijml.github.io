   The Installation 2008 Event   
  •    The Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association (LACAAEA) hosts the Installation 2008 Dinner on Thursday, July 31, 2008 at Ocean Star Seafood Restaurant, California.
  •    Reverend Terry Keenan, Judge Arthur M. Lew, Phillip Ow, Karl Nobuyuki, Henry Yee, Irene Hamada, Wendy Watanabe and Steve Kawamura make a speech at the event.
  •     Dr. Judy Chu received The George Ban Award for Public Service Excellence.
  •     Benjaman Chan, Albert Chu, Charlene Gomez and Andrew Trinh received Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association Shirley Bolinger Scholarship recipients for 2008.
