    •       Los Angeles, March 5, 2009 --    The Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association (LACAAEA) hosted a very memorable and enjoyable reception for five newly appointed department heads from the County of Los Angeles at Empress Pavilion Restaurant, Los Angeles, California, on Thursday evening, March 5, 2009.

    Phillip Chen, served as the master of ceremonies for the night. We would like to thank him for maintaining the event pace and keeping the program on track.

    William T Fujioka, CEO of the County of Los Angeles, nearly 20 department heads and other special guests also take their busy schedule to attend the "Meet and Greet" reception to honor five new department heads, as shown below.

    Irene Hamada, President of LACAAEA, has a welcome message: On behalf of the Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association (LACAAEA), I would like to welcome you to our 2009 “Meet and Greet” dinner reception. The LACAAEA officers and I appreciate your attendance and continued support!

    Please take the opportunity to meet and welcome our Honorees and special guests. We are very proud to present to you Dean C. Logan, Tom Tindall, Gail Farber, Santos H. Kreimann, and Wendy L. Watanabe, directors and managers representing some of the County’s leading departments. Enjoy!

    In the spirit of family and friendship LACAAEA wish new five department heads a very rewarding, productive, and successful career serving the people of Los Angeles County. Congratulations!
