Outgoing President's Message

It has been five years since I assumed the presidency of the Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association (LACAAEA). During that time, the Association has gone through a number of changes. Some of our members have retired (myself included) and some of our members are sadly no longer with us. In all of the 20 years of my membership, we have maintained our commitment to assist our members in their County careers. I wish our new President all of the best and I am confident that she will continue to make LACAAEA an active organization within the County and the community.
