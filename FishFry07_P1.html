      The 24th Annual Fish Fry 2007   

    Los Angeles, Sept. 29, 2007 -- The 24th annual "Fish Fry" was here on Saturday, Sept. 29, 2007, bringing together members of LACAAEA at Mother’s Beach, Marina del Rey, California.

Henry Yee, current LACAAEA President and Retiree Phillip Ow, former CAO employee and past LACAAEA President 1981 have been preparing delicious fish and seafood chowder for our members and guests for many years. They start early in the morning to prepare the delectable dishes for the enjoyment of all. The Fish Fry is a tradition with many of our members and families. Perhaps it will become a tradition with you as well. It is a time to see old friends, meet new ones, relax, enjoy the ocean air and sun, and have great food and perhaps win prizes. If you were unable to attend, you missed a great Event!

    Thank you Board of Supervisors & Department of Beaches & Harbors

    Los Angeles, Sept 29, 2007 -- The 24th Annual Fish Fry on Sept 29, 2007 is made possible with the assistance from Board of Supervisors and Department of Beaches and Harbors. For the past 24 years, LACAAEA members and families have been enjoying the festivities at Mother's Beach, Marina del Rey, California.

Many Executives and leaders from the County attended the event and LACAAEA members had an opportunity to meet and network with Executive management personnel during the event. Click here to view more photos.

                 Networking and Jobs

    Los Angeles, Sept 29, 2007 -- The 24th Annual Fish Fry on Sept 29, 2007 at Mother's Beach provided an opportunity to network and learn about new career opportunities.

Tae An, a manager at Auditor-Controller attended his first Fish Fry and realized that by becoming a LACAAEA member, he greatly increased his networking opportunities. After 27 years with the County, he now realizes the importance of the Association in furthering his career. Congratulations, Tae for becoming a LACAAEA member on September 29, 2007.
