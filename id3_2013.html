 Board Officers
LACAAEA was established in the 1970s. Currently, it consists of nine Board Officers and has more than 400 members.
One of the primary functions of our association is to give members opportunity to network and interact with other members and guests, including senior county management.
The Association's Board meets on the third Tuesday of each month. Members are encouraged to attend the Board meetings whenever they can and to offer suggestions and comments. To become more involved with our Association, please check the Calendar of Events, or call (213) 243-8113 to obtain information on upcoming functions, or leave a message in on the voice mail. We will get back to you shortly.
We invite you to visit us or attend an event. Our members are more than happy to answer any questions you may have about the association and how you can join.
