A WELCOME MESSAGE FROM THE PRESIDENT

Henry Yee, President of LACAAEA.
More photos >>
     On behalf of the Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association (LACAAEA), I would like to welcome you to our Fall 2007”Meet and Greet” reception. The LACAAEA officers and I appreciate your attendance and continued support!

Since the Summer 2007 "Meet and Greet", the LACAAEA successfully held, participated and supported various events, including the DCFS’ 12th Annual All for the Love of Kids Family Fundraiser and Awards Dinner, hosted by Supervisor Michael D. Antonovich (5th District), a reception honoring William T Fujioka, the new Los Angeles County CEO, sponsored by the Asian Pacific Islander Community Groups, the Hispanic Manager’s Association’s Special Reception Honoring William T Fujioka, and the 24th Annual Fish Fry, held at Mother’s Beach, Marina Del Rey.

I would like to extend an invitation to all our members and their guests to the upcoming New Officers Installation Dinner. For the remainder of my term, I would like to continue to expand our outreach among the Asian American employees employed by Los Angeles County and in the community. I look forward to seeing LACAAEA continue to grow and become a more prominent organization within the Los Angeles County family. I urge all of our members to attend the many events that our organization sponsors and meet the many others who serve the County and the community.

Please take the opportunity to meet and welcome our Honorees and special guests. We are very proud to present to you Philip Browning, Jonathan Fielding, Steven Golightly, and Gregg Rademacher, directors and managers representing some of the County’s leading departments. Enjoy!


Sincerely,


Henry Yee, President
