Jeremy Li, Web Master
View more photos >>
      Jeremy Li graduated cum laude from California state University, Northridge with a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering in 1987. He started to work at an aerospace company, Schaeffer Magnetics, Inc., Chatsworth, California, as electrical engineer. He was so proud of himself that he had made a small contribution to American's space program because the reaction wheel (he designed the printed circuit board) was used on the space flight in 1990's.

He gained experience under the guidance of the company owner, Ernie Schaffer, who became his mentor for more than 10 years. Mr. Li served as IT manager at Schaeffer Magnetics, Inc. (SMI) for 7 years.

After SMI was sold in 1998, Jeremy joined USC University, Hospital as a network lead engineer to support the entire hospital's IT operation. In 1999, he joined the Litton Industries Inc., a diversified defense and technology company, as a Senior Network Engineer to support the entire data center at Litton Corporate, Woodland Hills, CA. After Northrop Grumman's 2001 acquisition of Litton Industries Inc., the Litton Corporate was closed in Jan, 2002. Jeremy was hired as a consultant at Northrop Grumman IT sector from Feb, 2002 to Dec, 2002. In Feb, 2003, he was hired by the County of Los Angeles, Auditor Controller as a network consultant through an IT consulting company Staff Tech, Inc. He received the Employee of the Month in June 2004 (Award Certificate).

In March 2005, Jeremy became the fulltime employee at the County of Los Angeles, Auditor Controller and continued to perform the same duty when he was hired in Feb, 2003. He often mentions he is so proud to be able to bring vast and successful IT experience from a private sector to a public sector, and has enjoyed working at public sector since day one. He always says that he is lucky to be able to design, implement and maintain the entire Microsoft Active Directory network along with HP technologies from A to Z at an enterprise environment.

He always contributes his IT successful stories and experiences through continued IT education and certifications. He was certified as a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer for NT 4.0 (MCSE) in July 1998 and re-certified as a  Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer for Windows 2000 (MCSE) in Jan, 2002. He was also certified as Compaq Master Accredited Systems Engineer in May 1999 and re-certified as HP Master Accredited Systems Engineer (HP MASE) in 2002 and 2005 (Video Presentation of HP Certified Professional Program). He was also certified as a Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) in May, 2001.

He joined the Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association in August, 2006 and immediately produced the web pages of 5/18/2006 Meet and Greet for the association. During the 23rd Annual Fish Fry event on Sept 09, 2006, Jeremy was named Web Master for the association in front of all attendees. He is also an amateur photographer for the association.
