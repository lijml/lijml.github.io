LACAAEA's 28th Annual Fish Fry and Annual Scholarship Awards, Sept. 17, 2011
 LACAAEA'S 28th Annual Fish Fry
   The Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association (LACAAEA) hosted the 28th Annual Fish Fry 2011 on Saturday, September 17, 2010 at Marina del Rey, California.
   The Los Angeles County Asian American Employees Association (LACAAEA) would like to thank the Board of Supervisors' support for this event.

LACAAEA Annual Scholarship Awards
LACAAEA wishes each of the 2010 scholarship recipients the best in both their academic and professional careers. We are proud to be able to support these talented young people and wish them the very best.
   Tiffany Lue, received the Howard Takata Trade and Vocational Scholarship award.
   Jonathan Trinh, received the Shirley Bolinger Scholarship award.
   Lori Li, class of 2014 at USC Marshall School, received the Ed Lee Continuing Undergraduate Scholarship award.

Phillip Ow's 2011 Fish Fry Menu/Task List

    The 28th annual "Fish Fry" was held on Saturday, Sept. 17, 2011, bringing nearly 200 members of LACAAEA, their families and friends together at Mother's Beach, Marina del Rey, California. It was an unusually cool and cloudy day but the anticipation of the festivities to come for our 28th annual "Fish Fry" made it seem like it was the perfect weather. The Fish Fry has served, for many years, as a mechanism by the Association to thank all our members for their membership, constant support, and loyalty.

    LACAAEA officers and members arrived very early in the morning to prepare fresh cooked seafood for the Fish Fry. While Henry was setting up the coffee pot, Philip prepared a delicious breakfast for the officers and members present.

    Henry Yee and Phillip Ow, the head chefs, along with sous chefs Kenji Mayeda, Karen Tu, and Armando Garcia and Barbara Yu as the "Grill Master", once again prepared delicious fish dishes and seafood stew and barbeque chicken for everyone in attendance. The air was filled with intoxicating aromas each time the seafood stew pot was opened and as Phillip was cooking the fried fish and sweet and sour fish..

   As the final preparations of the meal were coming together, LACAAEA President Irene Hamada welcomed all our members and guests to our 27th Annual Fish Fry event. President Hamada recognized and thanked all of her Board Members who made this event possible. Special thanks were given to Jackie Guevarra who chaired this year's event, and to everyone else who helped make this event a huge success. She proceeded by inviting everyone to enjoy the delicious food.

After everyone had enjoyed the meal, the Scholarship Committee (Meav Ear, Chair, and Greg Sasaki) presented this year's scholarship winners and their families with their certificates and scholarship checks. Afterwards, the children played various games (e.g., water balloon toss, Simon Says, etc), followed by bingo and raffle games hosted by Jackie Guevarra, LACAAEA Vice President. Everyone in attendance had a great time!
