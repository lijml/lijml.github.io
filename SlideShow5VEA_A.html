    Phillip Chen, Board Deputy for Los Angeles County Supervisor Michael D. Antonovich, kindly invites Phillip Ow, former president of LACAAEA to join him on stage for a photo, while presenting a proclamation to David Nguyendo, President of VEALAC.

    A famous traditional Lion dance, in which performers mimic a lion's movements in a lion costume, is performed at the beginning of the ceremony, Jan. 25, 2008. The dance is traditionally accompanied by gongs, drums and firecrackers, representing the descent of good luck.

    Los Angeles County Employees (DPSS) sings a Vietnamese folk song during the 2008 Lunar New Year ceremony, Jan. 25, 2008.

    David Nguyendo, President of VEALAC, and the rest of the performers went to the stage to thanks for all attendees at the end of the 2008 Lunar New Year ceremony, Jan. 25, 2008.
